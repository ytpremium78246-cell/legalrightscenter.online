About Us

We provide free, independent legal information to help US residents understand personal injury claims, mass tort eligibility, and insurance rules.

📅 Last Reviewed: June 2026
✍ Reviewed By: Michael E. Vance, Esq.
Information Disclaimer: LegalRightsCenter.online is an independent information directory. The details on this page do not constitute legal advice, are not a substitute for a licensed attorney's advice, and do not create an attorney-client relationship. State laws vary and deadlines (statutes of limitations) are strict.

Our Mission

At LegalRightsCenter.online, our mission is simple: to make the civil justice system transparent, accessible, and understandable for average Americans. When a person is injured or facing an insurance dispute, they are often placed under sudden, immense stress. Navigating complex legal terms, insurance rules, and statutes of limitations can be overwhelming. We compile peer-reviewed, state-specific information to help you understand your options before you choose to contact a lawyer.

Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of legal editors, consumer advocates, and legal research writers. Every article we publish undergoes rigorous fact-checking against official state laws, court records, and regulatory announcements (such as the FDA, CDC, and FMCSA). To maintain transparency, we display detailed author bio cards on every page and disclose our sources clearly.

Our Funding Disclosure

LegalRightsCenter.online is free to browse. To maintain our editorial team and provide high-quality materials, we offer paid attorney directory listings and lead referral connections. However, these sponsorships do not dictate our editorial focus or compromise the accuracy of our legal summaries. We are independent legal guides.

Our Mission and Consumer Advocacy Standpoint

At LegalRightsCenter.com, we operate on a single, core belief: that access to reliable legal information is a fundamental right. In the wake of an accident, a severe medical diagnosis, or an insurance denial, individuals are thrust into a complex, adversarial civil justice system. Without a clear understanding of key legal doctrines—such as negligence, statutes of limitations, joint and several liability, and comparative fault—consumers are at a severe disadvantage. We act as an independent, transparent bridge, translating complex statutory language into clear, actionable guides so that you can make informed decisions about your future.

Organizational History and Evolution

LegalRightsCenter.com was established in 2024 by a coalition of veteran legal journalists and retired civil litigators who recognized a glaring gap in the online legal ecosystem. While the internet is saturated with advertising-heavy law firm websites, these platforms often gloss over the critical nuances of state-specific statutes or fail to explain the mathematical frameworks behind their settlement calculators. Our organization was structured from the ground up to prioritize comprehensive databases of state codes, detailed insurance subrogation guides, and step-by-step guides to the litigation process. Over the years, we have expanded our coverage to include mass tort multidistrict litigation (MDL) trackers and medical malpractice analysis, serving thousands of visitors monthly.

Our Multi-Tiered Review and Fact-Checking Process

To ensure our materials meet the highest standards of reliability and comply with Your Money or Your Life (YMYL) content requirements, we implement a strict three-stage editorial pipeline:

  • Primary Source Sourcing: Our writers only draft guides using official state civil codes, federal regulations (such as 49 CFR for commercial vehicles or 21 CFR for drug approvals), and appellate court opinions. We never rely on competitor blog posts or unverified legal summaries.
  • Editorial Verification: Our editorial board, led by experienced legal journalists, reviews all drafts to verify that code citations, deadlines, and damage cap figures are accurate and current.
  • Licensed Attorney Review: Every article is submitted to a member of our legal advisory board—licensed attorneys who review the text to verify its legal correctness and ensure it remains strictly informational, without offering or implying formal legal advice.

Our Transparent Funding and Referral Model

LegalRightsCenter.com is entirely free to browse. We do not charge subscription fees or gate our articles behind paywalls. To support our research staff, maintain our databases, and fund our interactive calculator tools, we partner with a select network of licensed law firms. These partners pay for placement in our lawyer directory or receive case evaluation inquiries when a user explicitly requests a free consultation.
Our Editorial Independence Guarantee: We maintain a strict "wall of separation" between our sponsorship team and our editorial board. No sponsor, advertising attorney, or lead partner has the authority to review, draft, edit, or censor any legal summary, statutory database, or calculator formula published on our platform. Our first and only loyalty is to the truth and to the consumer seeking accurate guidance.
